Lazily.AI Brand Guidelines
Version 1.0 Date: July 28, 2025
1. Brand Essence
At its core, Lazily.AI is about Effortless Intelligence. Our brand is built on the clever juxtaposition of being "lazy" in effort but powerful in results. We provide a smart, automated advantage to real estate professionals, allowing them to achieve maximum output with minimum friction.
• Mission: To eliminate the most tedious parts of real estate transactions through intelligent automation, giving our users back their most valuable asset: time.
• Vision: To be the indispensable contract engine for every real estate professional in Texas.
• Brand Personality: Clever, Confident, Efficient, Approachable, and Trustworthy.
2. Logo
Our brand is represented by two primary logo assets: the Mascot Logo and the Wordmark.
Mascot Logo
The full logo, including the sloth mascot, the name, and the tagline.
• Primary Use: Use the full mascot logo on the homepage hero section, social media profiles, and in marketing materials where brand personality is key.
• Clear Space: Always maintain a clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "L" in "LAZILY.AI". Do not crowd the logo with other elements.
Wordmark
The text-only version of the logo.
• Primary Use: Use the wordmark in the website navigation header, on documents, and in situations where a more compact, professional mark is required.
Mascot Icon
The sloth icon can be used on its own as a favicon or as a user avatar.
3. Color Palette
Our color palette is warm, professional, and inspired by the tones of coffee and natural materials, reflecting both productivity and approachability.
• Primary (Action Color)
• #D9A168 (Warm Tan)
• Usage: Primary buttons, links, and key calls-to-action.
• Secondary (Headings & Strong Accents)
• #4A3F3A (Deep Coffee Brown)
• Usage: Main headlines, important text, secondary buttons.
• Neutrals (The Foundation)
• #FCFAF8 (Paper White): Main backgrounds.
• #F6F3F1 (Light Beige): Card backgrounds, subtle contrast.
• #E1DCD8 (Border Beige): Borders and dividers.
• #8A817C (Muted Brown): Secondary text, icons, and muted details.
4. Typography
Our typography is clean, modern, and highly readable, ensuring a professional and user-friendly experience.
• Primary Typeface: Manrope
• Why: Manrope is a modern sans-serif font that is both elegant and highly
legible on screens. It's available on Google Fonts, making it easy to implement on the web.
• Headings (H1, H2, H3):
• Font: Manrope
• Weight: Bold (700)
• Color: #4A3F3A (Deep Coffee Brown)
• Body Text:
• Font: Manrope
• Weight: Regular (400)
• Color: #4A3F3A (Deep Coffee Brown)
• Links & Buttons:
• Font: Manrope
• Weight: Medium (500) or Bold (700)
5. Voice & Tone
The Lazily.AI voice is knowledgeable and confident, but also clever and a little playful. We are experts who don't take ourselves too seriously.
• Tagline: Effortlessly Done.
• Key Principles:
• Be Clear & Concise: Get straight to the point. Avoid jargon where possible.
• Be Benefit-Driven: Always focus on the outcome for the user (saving time,
reducing errors, closing more deals).
• Be Confident: We are the best at what we do. Our language should reflect
that.
• Be Clever, Not Corny: Use wit and wordplay (like "Stop Grinding. Start
Closing.") but avoid clichés and unprofessional humor.
Example Phrases:
• Instead of: "Our platform utilizes a proprietary algorithm to process documents."
• Use: "Our contract engine gets the job done instantly."
• Instead of: "Purchase more credits to continue."
• Use: "Need to replenish your credits? Right this way."
